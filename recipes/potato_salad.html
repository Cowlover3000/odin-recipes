Papaya salad but with mango instead of papaya

Description

a basic recipe for papaya salad, also known as Som Tum in Thai cuisine. Keep in mind that there are many variations of this dish, so you can adjust the ingredients and quantities to your taste.

Ingedrients

  • 1 small green papaya (underripe), peeled, seeds removed, and shredded
  • 2-3 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2-3 Thai bird's eye chilies, chopped (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 2-3 tablespoons roasted peanuts, coarsely chopped
  • 1-2 tablespoons dried shrimp (optional), soaked in warm water and drained
  • tablespoons long beans or green beans, cut into 2-inch pieces (substitute with snow peas if unavailable)

Dressing

  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind pulp or tamarind concentrate
  • 1 tablespoon palm sugar or brown sugar (adjust to taste)
  • Juice of 1-2 limes (adjust to taste)
